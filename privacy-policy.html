Privacy Policy

Welcome to Intok! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our messaging application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • Username (chosen by you)
  • Preferred language and country settings
  • Profile picture (if provided)

Messages and Communications

  • Text messages you send and receive
  • Voice messages (if you use this feature)
  • Message translations (temporarily processed)

Authentication Data

  • Google Sign-In information (if you choose to sign in with Google)
  • Apple Sign-In information (if you choose to sign in with Apple)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Intok service
  • Translate your messages to recipients' preferred languages
  • Enable real-time messaging between users
  • Improve our translation accuracy and service quality
  • Send you important service updates

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Intok:

  • OpenAI - For AI-powered message translation
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) - For secure cloud hosting and data storage
  • Google Sign-In - For optional authentication
  • Apple Sign-In - For optional authentication

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/WSS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Access controls and authentication

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Intok is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" below.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
