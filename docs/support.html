Frequently Asked Questions

How does automatic translation work?

When you send a message, our AI analyzes the text and translates it to the recipient's preferred language. The translation happens in real-time, so there's no delay in your conversation.

How do I change my preferred language?

Go to Settings in the app, then select your preferred language and country. All incoming messages will be translated to this language.

Are my messages private?

Yes! Your messages are encrypted in transit. We process messages only to provide translation services and do not share your conversations with third parties.

How do I create a group chat?

Tap the "New Chat" button, select multiple users, and start your group conversation. Each member will see messages in their preferred language.

How do I react to messages with emojis?

Long-press on any message to open the emoji picker. Select an emoji to add your reaction!

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at the email below with your request. We'll process account deletions within 30 days.

The translation doesn't seem accurate. What can I do?

AI translations are constantly improving but may not be perfect. For important communications, we recommend confirming with the recipient. You can also tap on a translated message to see the original text.
